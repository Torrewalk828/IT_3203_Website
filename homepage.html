Introduction

The World Wide Web has evolved dramatically since its creation in 1989 by Tim Berners-Lee. Today the web supports communication, education, entertainment, business, and innovation around the world.

Originally designed to help researchers share documents, the web quickly expanded into a global system connecting billions of users and devices.
